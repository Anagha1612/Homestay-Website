Things to look forward to during the stay

  • As our guest, the focus is on you, your likes, and your preferences. Such would be the hospitality where ever you turn to there is only largesse and warmth. We in Vaikundam Legacy strive to maintain a fine balance between luxury and giving people a peep into the rubber culture as well as reconnect with nature. Those who hold nature close to their heart will find bliss here. Where offbeat is the buzz word and discovery the way to go, Vaikundam Legacy provides you the answer. Here, you can get out of your comfort zone while staying comfortable all the time. We aim to make your stay as alive as possible. You can also experience nothingness and go for a short walk with the rubber trees for the company. Or you can kick back, take up a book, enjoy music, or simply sit back and enjoy nature in all its wilderness, away from the busy city life. Alternatively, if you are feeling adventurous, the rubber trees and the estate await you. Have a guided plantation tour and learn how the plants are nurtured and grown. Watch at close quarters how a rubber tree is tapped. A peep into a working rubber estate!
  • Luxury in the lap of nature.
  • Trekking to NADUKANI topmost point in the estate.
    • As the name suggests, once you reach the top, you can see miles and miles of the countryside in all its beauty spread before you. Enjoy the mesmerizing sight of Chittar Lake! The scenic beauty that you are treated to is well worth the steep climb!
  • Guided plantation tours, leisure walks amid nature.
  • A walk through the plantation is an enriching experience. You will come across small, natural waterfalls where you can picnic.
  • Aesthetically furnished rooms and glorious interiors.
  • Visits to Kanyakumari, Padmanabhapuram Palace can be arranged.
  • Visits to Trivandrum can also be arranged for sight seeing- Kovalam, Padmanabhaswamy temple, museum, zoo, Veli.
  • Our other facilities would include a Conference Hall and a delightful choice of board games and books.
