Rooms and Dining

The homestay features six tastefully designed suites with a beautiful view. Aesthetically furnished with all modern amenities, you will be tucked into comfort in our rooms. The rooms overlook the rubber trees from where you could get up in the mornings to the chirping of the birds and the bustle of a working rubber estate. Peek out of the room, lo behold, you would be treated with the sight of rubber trees being tapped! Each room has a sit-out leading to the outside. Here, you can sit, sipping your morning tea or coffee, watching the activities unfurl outside, or even have a leisurely read through the newspaper!
